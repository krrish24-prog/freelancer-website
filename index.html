What is Freelancing?

Freelancing allows individuals to work on a project-by-project basis rather than being permanently employed by a single company. It offers freedom, flexibility, and the chance to work on a variety of projects with different clients.

Benefits of Freelancing

  • Flexibility to work from anywhere
  • Ability to choose your projects
  • Opportunity to work with multiple clients
  • Potential for higher earnings
